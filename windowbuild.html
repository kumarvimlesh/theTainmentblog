All About WINDOWS 10 BUILD 18334 (19H1)

10 Feb 2019
By-Vimlesh Kumar

Microsoft Release the window 10 build 18334 to window insiders with inhancing the gaming on laptops.This is the 22nd preview releasing for testers, as part of the April 2019 Update (version 1903), and it brings some new improvements and features specially in the gaming.window10 18334 currently available to Fast ring members of the Windows Insider Program.There also will be a slew of fixes and improvements for keyboard, mouse, Settings app, Cortana, Search, Nearby sharing and language with some improvements for Narrator.

“We’re excited to bring technology tailor-made for gaming to Windows,” Microsoft’s Dona Sarkar writes, as if it were 1996 and we were testing WinG again. “Help us validate these systems work as expected, and play State of Decay for free (limited time only!). Just tell us about any problems you encounter with installing and launching the game.”

PlAY THE STATE OF DECAY ABSOLUTELY FREE
