WHAT CELLULAR MOBILE COMMUNICATION IS?

08:02:2019
By-Vimlesh Kumar

Communication technologies has provided society with a vast array of new communication capabilities.Communication became more powerful after the cellular mobile communication technology.The cellular mobile communication technology came in the use from the very beginning of 1978 and spread very fast throughout the world.Today mobile communication became the backbone of society.Number of mobile phone user start growing fastly,according to report each 8 people form 10 people use mobile phone.Both cellular technology and smartphone make human life more sophisticated and easy.

GENERATIONS OF CELLULAR MOBILE TECHNOLOGY

Nordic Mobile Telephone(1-G)

The beginning of cellular technology start with the introduction of first generation of wireless technology in the 1979.1-G technology use the digital signaling to connect to the radio towers in which the voice signals are modulated in the higher frequency while a phone call.

Global System Mobile Communication(2-G)

The second generation of cellular technology is the result of the modification in the first generation of cellular technology.The GSM technology commercially launched in the year of 1991. The main difference in 1-G and 2-G is the 2-G use the digital radio signaling while 1-G use analog radio signaling.GSM also introduce some new services such as SMS, MMS and data communication by packet data transport using General Packet Radio Services.After later 2-G also release 2.5-G which have better functionality over 2-G.

Universal Mobile Telecommunications Services(3-G)

Upgradation of 2-G and 2.5-G cellular communication technology gives the third generation of cellular communication technology.The first 3-G networks were introduce in the year of 1998.3-G technology provide a much better information transfer rate of 0.2Mbit/s and provide a much better access to the mobile internet, video call and mobile TV. 3.5-G and 3.75-G which are released by 3-G provide a better mobile broadband access of several Mbit/s to smart phones and modems which are used in laptops and computers.

LONG-TERM EVOLUTION(4-G)

4th generation of mobile cellular technology is the current most used technology which succeed over 3-G.4-G technology provide mobile web access, HD mobile TV,video conferencing,3-D TV,Gaming services,IP Telepohony,Cloud Computing and many more services with best quality.4-G increase the data transfer rate and capacity using different radio interface together with core network improvement.4-G technology come in use after 2008 and replace 3-G cellular technology within some years.Peak Data transfer rate for 4-G is many times greater than of 3-G.Peak download transfer rate for LTE(4-G) is 100Mbits/s and that of peak upload is 50Mbit/s.

An enhancement to LTE in 2013 also known as LTE-Advanced which was able to achieve a higher Data transfer rate of 1000Mbit/s for peak download and 500Mbit for peak upload.
