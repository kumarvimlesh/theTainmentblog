Google Stadia : The Future of Gaming

30 Mar 2019
By-Vimlesh Kumar

Worlds largest technical company 'Google' reveal its new cloud game streaming service 'Google Stadia' with the help of cloud gaming on 19 march 2019 at San francisco.Google decided to launch its subscription to first to some country only and then make it globally available in 2019 itself.


Google Stadia is going to be the future of gaming within some upcoming months of 2019.The main principle of Google Stadia is the Cloud Gaming i.e. all processing, computing,controlling and all other work of game mainly done in the 'Google Data Centers' present at many places across the world and provide game link to the game player .Player who is playing the game will able to see and play the game on the screen of their computer, mobile or any other device having access to the internet with 64 frames per seconds and having subscription to the Google Stadia.

The main advantage of Google Stadia is that you can play any game anywhere on any device and in any hardware.You even not need to download and update any game.Today many gamer are not able to play some good games just because they don't have a good PC or Laptop with very high graphics and very good hardware.Today almost all game require very good hardware and graphics devices to play any game.Now the problem of buying good devices which are very costly and can't be afford by many gamer who really love gaming, is solved by google stadia.all you need is just a good internet connection and any device.Game streamer can directly stream their video or can save their game from Google Stadia with quality of 4k 1080p.



Google also announce a game controller which is very unique and filled with many features to control your game remotely.It can directly connect to the device in which game is running.Google assistance button in controller will help gamers to take any help if any needed from google while playing game.It also provides button to capture the game at any time while playing game.With the help of controller gaming become more better.Without controller also you can play the game with help of mouse, keyboard or any other controller you have.
